About The Course

Network Like A boss and Land Your Dream Job in 45 Days course is a 30-minute self-paced course designed to build relationships that can enable you to land your dream job to secure the bag and start living that rich aunty life you’ve always dreamed of.

Identify and Explore Proven Strategies

The course will help you identify and explore proven strategies on how to set and slay your goals, how to craft a compelling personal brand, and how to network like a pro to land your dream job.

Pass Me The Mic Course with Darling

About The Course

About The Course“Pass Me the Mic” is a free, 30-minute, online course created to help young African women learn communication skills to advance their careers. This course is a fast, free, self-guided course designed to help build your communication skills for work.

Identify and Explore Proven Strategies

With the Pass Me The Mic course, you will learn:

  • How to craft your professional brand
  • How to communicate your worth assertively, pitching your values, especially during job interviews and also while negotiating your salary
  • Smart negotiating strategies to get the best job offer!
