SLA BoostHer Program

The SLA BoostHer Program, in collaboration with Jobberman is a
transformative initiative designed to empower young Nigerian
women entrepreneurs. This program addresses the unique
challenges faced by women in business and job-seeking through a
blended learning experience that includes advanced self-paced
courses. Through a blended learning approach, participants will gain
access to high-quality training, expert-led masterclasses, and hands-
on guidance tailored to their needs, fostering sustainable business
growth and economic independence.
